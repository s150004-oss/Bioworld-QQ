Laboratory Safety Guidelines

Personal Protection

Always wear lab coats, safety goggles, and gloves when handling biological specimens or chemicals.

No Food or Drink

Never eat, drink, or store food in the laboratory to prevent contamination and ingestion of hazardous materials.

Hand Hygiene

Wash hands thoroughly before and after laboratory work, especially when handling biological materials.

Microscope Guide

Proper Focusing

Always start with the lowest magnification objective when focusing.

Measurement Techniques

Use stage micrometer and eyepiece graticule for accurate cell measurements.

Optimal Lighting

Adjust diaphragm and condenser for proper illumination and contrast.
